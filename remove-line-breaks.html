Remove Line Breaks

Easily remove all line breaks or replace them with spaces from your text.

What does this tool do?

This tool removes all line breaks (newlines) from your text or replaces them with spaces, depending on the option you choose.

It's useful when you're copying text from PDFs, emails, or chat logs that include unwanted line breaks, and you want to clean it up for reuse.

  • Remove completely: Deletes all newline characters to merge text into one line.
  • Replace with space: Preserves spacing between lines without hard breaks.

Textory processes your input in real-time in your browser – nothing is uploaded or stored.
