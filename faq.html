General Questions

What is Textory?

Textory is a free collection of online text processing tools designed for writers, developers, and content creators. Our tools include a word counter, case converter, line break remover, and JSON formatter, all running locally in your browser for privacy and speed.

Is Textory really free?

Yes, all our text tools are completely free to use with no limits on usage. We may display advertisements to support the service, but all core functionality remains free forever.

Do I need to create an account?

No account registration is required. Simply visit any tool page and start using it immediately. This keeps things simple and protects your privacy.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. All text processing happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your content to our servers. Your data stays on your device at all times.

Tool-Specific Questions

How accurate is the Word Counter?

Our word counter uses advanced algorithms to accurately count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs. It properly handles contractions, hyphenated words, and various punctuation marks. The tool also filters out common stop words when showing top word frequencies.

What text formats does the Case Converter support?

The Case Converter supports four formats: UPPERCASE (all capitals), lowercase (all small letters), Capitalize (first letter of each word), and Sentence case (first letter of each sentence). Changes are applied instantly as you type or select different options.

When should I use the Line Break Remover?

This tool is perfect for cleaning up text copied from PDFs, emails, or chat applications that often introduce unwanted line breaks. You can choose to remove line breaks completely or replace them with spaces to maintain readability.

Does the JSON Formatter validate my JSON?

Yes, our JSON formatter automatically validates your JSON syntax and highlights any errors. It can beautify valid JSON with proper indentation or minify it to remove all unnecessary whitespace. The tool also includes line numbers for easy debugging.

Technical Questions

Which browsers are supported?

Textory works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience. The tools also work well on mobile devices.

Do the tools work offline?

While you need an internet connection to initially load the pages, all text processing happens locally in your browser. Once a tool is loaded, it will continue to work even if your internet connection is temporarily interrupted.

Is there a limit to how much text I can process?

There are no artificial limits imposed by Textory. The only limitations are your device's memory and browser capabilities. Our tools are optimized to handle large documents efficiently, but extremely large files may slow down processing on older devices.

Can I use Textory for commercial projects?

Yes, you can use our tools for both personal and commercial projects. There are no restrictions on the type of content you process, as long as it complies with our Terms of Service and applicable laws.
