About Textory

Why We Built Textory

In our daily work as writers, developers, and content creators, we constantly found ourselves needing simple text utilities. Counting words for essays, converting text cases for coding projects, cleaning up messy formatted text from PDFs, or formatting JSON data for debugging. The existing tools were either cluttered with ads, slow to load, or lacked the precision we needed.

That's why we created Textory — a clean, fast, and reliable collection of text tools that just work. No unnecessary features, no data collection, just powerful utilities that help you get your work done efficiently.

Our Mission

We believe that essential text tools should be fast, free, and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student writing an essay, a developer debugging API responses, or a content creator optimizing social media posts, Textory provides the precision and speed you need.

Everything runs locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and lightning-fast performance.

Our Tools

Word Counter

Advanced text analysis with word, character, sentence, and paragraph counting. Features intelligent word frequency analysis and stop-word filtering for writers and researchers.

Case Converter

Instantly transform text between uppercase, lowercase, capitalize, and sentence case. Perfect for normalizing content, creating titles, or formatting code documentation.

Line Break Remover

Clean up text copied from PDFs, emails, or chat logs by removing unwanted line breaks. Choose to remove completely or replace with spaces for better readability.

JSON Formatter

Professional JSON beautifier and validator with syntax highlighting, line numbers, and error detection. Essential for developers working with APIs and data structures.

Built for Privacy & Performance

Textory is designed with privacy and performance at its core. All text processing happens directly in your browser — we never see, store, or transmit your data. This approach ensures your sensitive content stays private while delivering instant results without server delays.

Our clean, modern interface works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices, with full dark mode support for comfortable use in any environment.
