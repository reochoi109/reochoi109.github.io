Case Converter

Convert your text to various text cases in real time.

What does this case converter do?

This tool helps you quickly convert your text into different letter cases: uppercase, lowercase, capitalized, and sentence case.

It's ideal for writers, editors, and developers who need to normalize text formatting across documents, content, or code.

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals
  • lowercase: Converts everything to lowercase
  • Capitalize: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence

Changes are made instantly in your browser — no upload, no waiting.
